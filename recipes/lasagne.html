My Delicious Vegetarian Lasgane

My personal favourite lasagne recipe

This recipe is my go to for feeding the family, it's super delicious and packed full of fresh ingredients!

The recipe is one of my favourite recipes from when I was growing up.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 8 Lasagne Sheets
  • 450g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 Small Aubergine
  • 2 Medium Courgettes
  • 1 Yellow and 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 3 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Capers
  • 2 Tablespoons of Torn Basil Leaves
  • 75g Grated Mozarella

For the Sauce:

  • 35g Plain Flour
  • 40g Butter
  • 600ml Milk
  • 1 Bayleaf
  • Freshly Grated Nutmeg
  • 3 Tablespoons of Grated Vegetarian Parmesan


Method

  1. Cut the aubergine and courgette into 2.5cm dice and toss with a dessertspoon of salt.
  2. Pack the aubergine and courgette into a colander and use a weight to compress and drain the liquid. Leave for approximately 1 hour.
  3. Arrange the aubergine, courgette, tomatoes and onion in a roasting tin. Sprinkle with chopped garlic, basil and olive oil to coat.
  4. Roast the vegetables for 30-40 minutes at 220°c.
  5. During this; make the sauce by first melting butter in a saucepan and whisking in the flour.
  6. Incorporate the milk into the butter/flour mix gradually whilst whisking to ensure a smooth sauce.
  7. Add most of the parmesan (leaving some for sprinkling on top of the lasagne) to the sauce along with the grated nutmeg and bay leaf.
  8. Allow the sauce to infuse and thicken by bringing to the boil and turning the heat down to the lowest setting for 2 minutes.
  9. Once the sauce and vegetables are done, create the lasagne by alternating layers of sauce, vegetables, mozarella and the lasagne sheets.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining parmesan over the top of the lasagne.
  11. Bake the lasagne in the oven for 30 minutes at 180°c or until the top is browned and crispy.
