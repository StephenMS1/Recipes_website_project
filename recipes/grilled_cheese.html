My French Onion Grilled Cheese

Comfort food 101

This recipe is an excellent comfort food for when you just want a quick and delicious snack.

My girlfriend loves this recipe so much I have to make it weekly!

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Red Onion - sliced/li>
  • 4 Slices Tiger Bread
  • 150g Grated Cheddar
  • 2 Teaspoons of Dijon Mustard
  • 75g Butter


Method

  1. Caramelise the onions by sautéing for 5 minutes and then turning the temperature down and cooking for a further 15 minutes.
  2. Spread the mustard across the bread slices which will face the internal of the sandwhich.
  3. Apply a thin layer of butter to the bread slices which will face the outside of the sandwhich.
  4. Place the grated cheddar into the sandwhich and top with the caramelised onions followed by more cheddar.
  5. Fry both the outsides of the sandwhich to create a crispy outer shell.
  6. Once the outside is sufficiently goldened, place the sandwhiches into an oven, pre-heated to 200°c, until the cheese is fully melted.
